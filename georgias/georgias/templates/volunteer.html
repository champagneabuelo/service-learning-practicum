Volunteer Today

Want to help? We would love it. There are many needs Georgia's Healing House has and if you can be a dedicated volunteer we would like to meet you.

Do you have a special talent that can help women in recovery? Maybe teach a class in nutrition and exercise, budgeting and saving, art or writing.

How about hosting a fund raising event or going to stores and setting up fresh produce and other food donations. Use your imagination there is so much you can do!
